Bulking Bars

Even better, you can make these at home in five minutes WITHOUT cooking for 1/10 the cost of pre-made protein bars and weight gain products.

Ingredients

  • Blended oats 200g
  • Powdered Milk 200g
  • Peanut Butter 200g
  • Maple Syrup/Honey 200g
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Mix peanut butter, syrup and cocoa
  2. Add powdered milk to mix, add some water if needed but not too much as you want it to stay together
  3. Finally add in oats to and mix it up. Again you can add a little bit of water if its not mixing well but the finished product should be firm
  4. Get a square pan or container and line it with a non stick baking paper
  5. Proceed to lay out your soon to be bulking bars in the container and place another sheet of baking paper on top
  6. Use a can or any other cylinder shaped object to roll out it out untill its even across the container
  7. Place in fridge over night. Cut it up in even pieces the next day
